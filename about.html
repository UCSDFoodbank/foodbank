About Us

Hi, we are Foodbank!
Our mission is to help combat food waste and feed the hungry.
We provide a comprehensive database for those looking for food, and for those who are giving out food.
Charities, food services, and individuals are all encouraged to give us a try!
Here at Foodbank, we believe in the power of food, and with the help of everyone,
we can do something truly amazing. Foodbank--Connections through food.
